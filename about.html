About Us

MSAS is established in 2014, in JP Nagar the south central of bangalore.! The journey started with a few student who wanted to express themselves through music which chained up hundreds of music appreciators, listeners, music lover to join us in this journey to learn and play music in multiple venues of jp nagar.!we have not only helped to grow them with music but also have successfully created different platforms where they perform with other professional musicians and artist which help them to build confidence in facing the stage audience and learn cordination with other musicians.

Our aim is to bring the best musician out of you, to feed the increasing demand of musicians in this industry. Whether you are a complete novice, a hobbyist or want to serve music professionally. We work at your current skill level and advance you from their.!!
MSAS is the door which opens the diverse possibilities of music to happen with you. We believe music as the sound of every soul.
